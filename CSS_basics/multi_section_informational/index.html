The Pink City of India

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City for its unique-colored buildings. It is famous for its royal heritage, forts, culture, and vibrant markets.

Rich History

Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is one of India’s first planned cities. Its architectural beauty and grid-style layout attract historians and tourists from all over the world.

Delicious Rajasthani Cuisine

From Dal Baati Churma to Ghewar, Jaipur offers mouth-watering dishes full of flavor and tradition. Every meal is a celebration of Rajasthan’s culture and warmth.

Famous Attractions

Some of the most popular places to visit in Jaipur include the Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar. Each monument tells a story of art, royalty, and grandeur.
